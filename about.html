How It Works

The Trading Capital Calculator takes into account several key parameters:

  • Monthly profit target
  • Risk per trade percentage
  • Number of trades per month
  • Expected win rate
  • Risk-reward ratio

Using these inputs, it calculates the required trading capital needed to achieve your monthly goals while maintaining proper risk management principles.
